Instructions to Join Hostel

  • Students must submit valid college ID.
  • Hostel admission form must be completed.
  • Room will be allocated after fee payment.
  • Students must follow hostel discipline.

Hostel Rules

  • No electric cooking items allowed.
  • No heaters or induction stoves.
  • No alcohol or drugs.
  • No fire or flame items.
  • Maintain cleanliness in rooms.
  • Silence must be maintained after 10 PM.
